# BVZyme TG881®
## Bakery Enzyme – Technical Data Sheet

---

## Product Identification

- **Product name**: BVZyme TG881®  
- **Category**: Bakery Enzyme  
- **Enzyme preparation based on**: Transglutaminase  
- **Producer**: VTR & Beyond  

---

## Product Description

BVZyme TG881® is transglutaminase which produced by fermentations a selected strain.

---

## Physicochemical Properties

- **Aspect**: Free flowing powder  
- **Color**: White – white cream  
- **Moisture**: < 15 %  

---

## Effective Material

- **Enzyme**: Transglutaminase  
- **Activity**: 200 U / g  

---

## Application

BVZyme TG881® is used in bakery as a strong protein cross-linking (connecting residues of the amino acid L-glutamine to the amino acid L-lysine).

---

## Function

- Improve volume  
- Improve texture  
- Improve dough elasticity  
- Increase strength  
- Improve stability and tolerance  

---

## Dosage

- **Recommended dosage**: 10 – 40 ppm
