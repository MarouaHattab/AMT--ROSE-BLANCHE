# BVZyme AF SX®
## Bakery Enzyme – Technical Data Sheet

---

## Product Identification

- **Product name**: BVZyme AF SX®  
- **Category**: Bakery Enzyme  
- **Enzyme preparation based on**: Amylase  
- **Producer**: VTR & Beyond  

---

## Product Description

BVZyme AF SX® is an enzyme preparation based on amylase.

---

## Effective Material

- **Enzyme**: Fungal α-amylase  
- **Origin**: Produced by the fermentation of a selected strain of *Aspergillus oryzae*  
- **Activity**: 85 000 SKB / g  

---

## Application

BVZyme AF SX® is used in baking as it acts on damaged starch produced during the milling process by hydrolysis, producing sugars that aid in fermentation.

---

## Function

- Increase volume  
- Improve gassing power  
- Enhance softness  
- Assist in fermentation  

---

- **Recommended dosage**: 5 – 25 ppm
