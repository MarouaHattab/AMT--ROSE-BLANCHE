# BVZyme HCF600®
## Bakery Enzyme – Technical Data Sheet

---

## Product Identification

- **Product name**: BVZyme HCF600®  
- **Category**: Bakery Enzyme  
- **Enzyme preparation based on**: Endo-xylanase  
- **Producer**: VTR & Beyond  

---

## Product Description

BVZyme HCF600® is an enzyme preparation based on endo-xylanase.  
Fungal xylanase produced by fermenting a selected unique strain of *Aspergillus niger*, with the application of modern biotechnology.

---

## Effective Material

- **Enzyme**: Fungal xylanase  
- **Activity**: 7750 XylH / g  

---

## Application

BVZyme HCF600® is used in bakery and bread applications.

---

## Function

- Optimize gluten extensibility and gas retention  
- Improve oven spring and loaf volume  
- Increase volume  
- Increase tolerance  
- Improve baking performance of flour  
- Improve stability  

---

## Dosage

- **Standardization of wheat flour**: 1 – 5 ppm  
- **Bread improvement**: 2 – 35 ppm  
- **Suggested optimum dosage**: 2 – 10 ppm
