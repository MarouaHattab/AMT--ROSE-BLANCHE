# BVZyme A FRESH202®
## Bakery Enzyme – Technical Data Sheet

---

## Product Identification

- **Product name**: BVZyme A FRESH202®  
- **Category**: Bakery Enzyme  
- **Enzyme preparation based on**: Maltogenic Amylase  
- **Producer**: VTR & Beyond  

---

## Product Description

BVZyme A FRESH202® is an amylolytic enzyme system prepared from selected strains of *Bacillus subtilis*.

---

## Physicochemical Properties

- **Aspect**: Free flowing powder  
- **Color**: White – cream  
- **Moisture**: < 15 %  

---

## Effective Material

- **Enzyme**: Maltogenic amylase  
- **Activity**: 10950 NMAU / g  

---

## Application

BVZyme A FRESH202® is designed to improve the freshness of bread.

---

## Function

- Improve freshness  
- Enhance softness  
- Extend shelf life  

---

## Dosage

- **Recommended dosage**: 10 – 90 ppm  

---
