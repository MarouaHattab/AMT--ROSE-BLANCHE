# BVZyme AMG880®
## Bakery Enzyme – Technical Data Sheet

---

## Product Identification

- **Product name**: BVZyme AMG880®  
- **Category**: Bakery Enzyme  
- **Enzyme preparation based on**: Amyloglucosidase  
- **Producer**: VTR & Beyond  

---

## Product Description

BVZyme AMG880® is a fungal amyloglucosidase produced by the fermentation of a selected strain of *Aspergillus niger*.

---

## Physicochemical Properties

- **Aspect**: Free flowing powder  
- **Color**: White – cream  
- **Moisture**: < 15 %  

---

## Effective Material

- **Enzyme**: Fungal amyloglucosidase (glucoamylase)  
- **Activity**: 70 000 AGI / g  

---

## Application

BVZyme AMG880® is a glucoamylase that hydrolyzes (1,4) glucosidic linkages at the non-reducing ends of polysaccharides.

---

## Function

- Improve crust color to golden brownish  
- Improve crumb color brightness  
- Improve oven spring  
- Improve fermentation  

---

## Dosage

- **Recommended dosage**: 10 – 100 ppm
